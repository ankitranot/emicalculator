Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 7, 2025

Data We Do Not Collect

The EMI Calculator is designed to operate entirely in your web browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit the personal financial data (loan amount, interest rate, or tenure) you enter into the calculator fields.

Information Collection and Use

We do not require you to provide any personal information (such as name, email, or contact details) to use the core calculator function.

Cookies and Tracking

This website may use standard third-party services (like web analytics) that utilize cookies to track general site usage and performance, but these are not tied to your calculator inputs.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
